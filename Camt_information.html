College of Arts, Media and Technology (CAMT)

About CAMT

CAMT is a college of Chiang Mai University that focuses on technology, creativity, and innovation. It offers programs in software engineering, digital games, digital media, and related fields.

Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Digital Games
  • Bachelor of Science in Digital Media
